Why support ethical AI?

As techonology continues to dominate the world we live in today, the creation of ethical AI remains a pressing concern. Although it is easy to associate ethical AI with an imminent sci-fi robot apocalypse, this idea is better left in Hollywood. At the moment, AI has so much potential in fields such as the medical industry, business, etc. However, it is important to consider how AI constantly infringes on our privacy rights (ex. social media apps using your data without your permission) or even how fake news and disinformation campaigns are constantly circulated through AI-powered Bot accounts. Other issues with AI include what it takes to define a 'good outcome'.


AI differs significantly from traditional computers that base off calculations on mathematical principles. AI can be suited to fit a variety of needs, but it is always based on a prebuilt coding language. A result is either one or the other. For instance, if we took a look at an algorithm that analyzes how happy someone is, we would need to train the algorithm on every single combination of race, gender, and age possible. Even if this was done, how could we keep a researcher's definition of happiness from biasing results? How do we even know what happiness looks like? Bias is a significant problem in aritifical intelligence, since it is impossible to create a completely unbiased machine learning algorithm. This is what makes AI ethics so important.

How can I be more involved?

You can, of course, become a learner and attend our in-person/virtual events or apply to be a volunteer through our volunteering application.

You can also start a MakerSafe chapter at your school and submit articles to the MakerSafe blog! Contact us for details.

What are the benefits of becoming a member?

Members have access to an amazing educational community here at MakerSafe, where they can teach others and learn more about STEM. They gain opportunities to learn about cutting-edge research and conduct projects themselves under the guidance of our highly-experienced MakerSafe mentors.

We will be working on our status so that members can get volunteer hours for the incredible work that they do! In addition, being a chapter president of a MakerSafe chapter is another great way to build leadership skills.

Where was MakerSafe formed?

MakerSafe was formed in Fair Lawn, New Jersey by a group of highly-motivated high school students. We are hoping to expand on the national and international level. If you are interested in starting a MakerSafe chapter in your community, feel free to contact us for guidelines.

How do you start a MakerSafe chapter?

MakerSafe chapters will gain all MakerSafe learning materials and guides. Members will have their usual special benefits, which will include invitations to events, networking with guest speakers, and the opportunity to gain volunteering experience.

MakerSafe chapter leaders can also become a part our executive board.

What kind of events does MakerSafe host?

MakerSafe hosts ethical AI and programming workshops, which are taught by our highly-experienced MakerSafe mentors and educators. We also host computer science competitions and programming camps for middle school and high school students. Stay tuned to hear about our first MakerSafe hack-a-thon!
